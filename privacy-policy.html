Privacy Policy

ReadUnlock - Children's Reading App

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

ReadUnlock stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data stored on-device includes:

  • Reading progress, streaks, and time spent reading
  • Comprehension quiz scores
  • The parent PIN (stored securely in the device Keychain)
  • App settings such as reading level and daily goal
  • The parent email address you optionally provide for notifications

Screen Time and Family Controls

ReadUnlock uses Apple's Family Controls framework to apply app restrictions. This requires authorization from the device's Screen Time settings.

We do not have access to which specific apps are installed on the device. Apple's framework handles this privately using opaque tokens that are never transmitted to us.

Subscriptions and Payments

Purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

Subscription management (cancellation, renewal) is done through your Apple ID settings. After a 3-day free trial, the subscription auto-renews at $4.99/month unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

Children's Privacy

ReadUnlock is designed for use by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

No account creation is required, and no data from a child's use of the app leaves the device.

Email Notifications

If you optionally provide a parent email address, it is stored locally on your device. When you enable email notifications, ReadUnlock uses EmailJS (emailjs.com) to deliver those emails.

Your email address and the notification content are transmitted to EmailJS's servers solely for the purpose of sending the email. EmailJS does not store your email address beyond what is necessary to deliver the message.

You can disable notifications at any time in Parent Settings.

Third-Party Services

ReadUnlock uses the following third-party service:

  • EmailJS (emailjs.com) - used to send optional parent notification emails

No analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking services are used in this app.

Data Security

The parent PIN is stored in the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted and sandboxed to this app. All other data is stored in the app's private local storage and is not accessible to other apps.
